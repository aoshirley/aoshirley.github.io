So first things first... there is no A.O. Shirley.  The name is a combination of two grandparents.  Grandpa Gallant was a kind and generous man who lived humbly and gave where he can, he passed away before ever seeing the foundation take flight.  Grandma Shirley was a beam of light that shone wherever she was.  Truly one of the kindest souls 
who always saw the best in others.  Grandma Shirley was more interested in the smiles of others versus having her name memorialized.


Founded in 1995...  
The A.O. Shirley Memorial Foundation was founded on the principle of offering a hand up not a hand out to those individuals and groups who are making a meaningful difference in 
quality of life in our community.  
A passion for the arts and our youth is at the heart of the Foundation. 
The primary goal is to enrich lives through inspirational experiences.
At the heart of our Foundation is the understanding that allowing people to be their own hero in their own world outdoes any efforts we can make.

The AO Shirley Foundation has a 20 year history of starting up socialpreneurial projects.  We do the heavy lifting in the first 3 years by…

     - establishing a sustainable financial path
     - create truly full circle blessed partnerships
     - ensure the project gets on its feet
     - support lit up volunteers to establish a new board 

and then we release the project and go out and start another project.
